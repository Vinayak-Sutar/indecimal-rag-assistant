# One Piece: The Grand Line Compendium

## The Devil Fruits

Devil Fruits are mystical fruits found throughout the world that, when consumed, provide the eater a special super-human power, at the cost of losing the ability to swim.

They are categorized into three types:

- **Paramecia:** The most common type. They give the user super-human physical abilities or traits. (e.g., Luffy's Gum-Gum Fruit, which turns his body into rubber).
- **Zoan:** Allows the user to transform into an animal, as well as a hybrid form of their own species and the animal species. (e.g., Chopper's Human-Human Fruit).
- **Logia:** The rarest type. Allows the user to transform their body into a natural element, making them largely immune to physical attacks without Haki. (e.g., Ace's Flame-Flame Fruit).

## The Four Emperors (Yonko)

The Four Emperors are the four most powerful pirate captains in the world. They reside in the second half of the Grand Line, known as the New World. The Marines and the Seven Warlords of the Sea exist largely to counterbalance their immense power.
The original four shown in the series were:

1. Whitebeard (Edward Newgate) - Known as the strongest man in the world.
2. Big Mom (Charlotte Linlin) - Ruler of Totto Land with soul-manipulation powers.
3. Kaido - A massive pirate capable of turning into a mythical dragon.
4. Shanks - The red-haired pirate who inspired Luffy to set sail.

## The Will of D.

Throughout history, certain individuals are born with the middle initial "D" in their name. Very little is known about the meaning of the D, but those who bear it are said to be the natural enemies of the Gods (The Celestial Dragons). Notable carriers include Monkey D. Luffy, Trafalgar D. Water Law, and Gol D. Roger.

## Bounties

The World Government issues bounties on individuals they deem a threat. Bounties are measured in a currency called "Berries". A high bounty does not strictly mean a pirate is powerful, but rather how much of a threat they pose to the government's operations.
